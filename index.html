Have you too been scammed by HEG?

Join thousands of others who've been scammed by HappyEasyGo, raise some voice and bring them down.

A couple of months back, I made a flight booking in HappyEasyGo(HEG) which got cancelled by the airlines. So I made a request for cancellation and refund in the HEG website and I was asked to wait for 30-45 days for the airlines to refund the amount. However, within a week, I got a mail from the airlines stating that they had completed refund to the travel agency (HEG). 72 days has passed since I requested for refund and I got no refund, no replies to emails and no answers to calls. I decided to take it to twitter hoping some executives from HEG would see and take action but turns out there's someone like me complaining about HEG on twitter almost every 10 mins. There were countless people facing similar issues but not one reply from HEG's official account. The company is clearly fraud and I don't they'll ever respond to us or return our money but hey, let's atleast do something to bring this to the attention of concerned authorities and future customers!
